About Us

The HospiKol system facilitates direct communication between the child with severe intellectual disabilities and their parents. The system sends specified requests that are triaged and delegated to the parent to give their child what they requested for.

"An app worth alot I've been using it since I gave birth and now i can communicate with my 7 years old son. I'm really grateful for it." - Jennifer Da-Vinci

Communication

The app will help the children communicate using simple understandable pictures, these pictures will display basic ways of communication.

Notifications

The parents will get the notifications their child is trying to send and give them whatever they requested for.
